Is a Crayfish right for me?

Here's a List

  • One Tank
    • For every 2 crayfish your tank should have 1 square foot of space
  • Aquariaum Gravel
    • Small gravel is best for burrowing
  • Small freshwater plants for hiding
  • Rocks small or large
    • Water should be kept shallow unless there are large rocks for climbing into higher areas
    • Small rocks are good for hiding behind
  • Water conditionar/dechlorinator
  • Food
    • Pet stores will have crayfish or lobster food available both are good
    • They can also eat small bits of shrimp or earthworms
  • Cleaning brush and Vacuum
