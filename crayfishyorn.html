Things to Remember

  • Crayfish are not active during the day, don't worry if you don't see them out!
  • Their care needs are different then cats and dogs but no less time consuming.
  • They need enrichment like any other animal! No boring tanks!
  • Like any pet, owning crayfish can be incredibly rewarding as long as you are prepared to give them the care they need.
