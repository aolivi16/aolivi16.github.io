This website is intended to teach people all about crayfish, also known as crawfish, and show everyone that these little creatures make great pets. I owned a pet crayfish for over 6 years and enjoyed every minute of it! And I hope that this website will reach people interested in having an uncommon pet. Whether you are just browsing the website for fun, or looking to purchase your own underwater friend, I hope this website will be able to teach you all about the simple and wonderful crayfish.
