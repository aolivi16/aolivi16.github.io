Is a Crayfish right for me?

  1. They are fresh water crustaceans
  2. Scientific name Procambarus Clarkii
  3. They molt several times a year
  4. They are related to crabs and lobsters
  5. They are the state crustacean of Lousiana
  6. They can be blue, gray or red depending on the species and environment
  7. They have several names and nicknames including Crawdads and Mudbugs
  8. They are omnivores just like humans
  9. They can have quite the personality when kept as pets!
